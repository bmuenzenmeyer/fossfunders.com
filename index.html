What?

We are a group of companies working together to make it weird not to fund Free and Open Source Software (FOSS).

Where We Focus

Not all projects want or need money. Of those that do, we pay special attention to developer productivity projects: languages, libraries, frameworks, editors, command-line tools, and the like.
